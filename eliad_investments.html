About the Company

More Than an Investment Firm

Eliad Investments & Securities is an ever-growing financial services firm committed to empowering Nigerians to achieve their financial goals. With a focus on sustainable wealth creation, we offer a comprehensive range of personalized services — financial advisory, investment portfolio management, fixed returns savings plans, and wealth management.

We are more than just an investment firm. We are dedicated to fostering economic empowerment through the democratization of financial knowledge for sustainable wealth creation. Our commitment to equipping individuals with the knowledge and tools to make informed financial decisions is a cornerstone of our mission.

Providing accessible financial solutions through technology
Promoting financial literacy of the public
Professional investment management
Long-term wealth creation for all Nigerians
